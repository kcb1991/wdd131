Legendary Story Arcs

The Death of Superman

Published in 1992, this arc shocked readers as Superman faced Doomsday in a brutal battle that led to his death. It explored themes of sacrifice and legacy.

All-Star Superman

Written by Grant Morrison, this 12-issue series is a poetic and philosophical take on Superman’s final days, celebrating his humanity and heroism.

For the Man Who Has Everything

A classic story by Alan Moore where Superman is trapped in a dream world where Krypton never exploded. It explores desire, loss, and identity.
