Legendary Creators

Jerry Siegel & Joe Shuster

The original creators of Superman in 1938. Their vision launched the superhero genre and changed pop culture forever.

John Byrne

Revitalized Superman in the 1980s with “The Man of Steel” reboot, redefining Clark Kent’s humanity and strength.

Dan Jurgens

Known for “The Death of Superman” storyline, Jurgens brought emotional depth and epic scale to the character’s mythos.

Zack Snyder

Director of modern Superman films, including “Man of Steel” and “Justice League,” offering a bold, cinematic interpretation.
