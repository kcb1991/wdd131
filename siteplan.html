Superman Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Superman Legacy Online

This name captures both the timelessness of the character and the idea of an ever-evolving hero. It's easy to remember and fits the tone of a fan-focused website highlighting Superman’s journey through comics, film, and pop culture.

Optional domain availability: supermanlegacy.net

Site Purpose

This site is dedicated to exploring the mythology, values, and cultural impact of Superman. Visitors will find timelines of his origin and transformations, tributes to creators, themed activities, and curated content celebrating Superman’s message of hope and justice.

Scenarios

  • What are Superman’s major story arcs throughout comic history?
  • Where can I watch the best Superman movies and shows ranked by fans?

Color Schema

  • Royal Blue (#003366): Used for headings and navigation elements to reflect Superman’s iconic suit and boldness.
  • Crimson Red (#e30613): Accent color for buttons, logos, and highlights to evoke Superman’s cape and heroic spirit.

Typography

  • Roboto Slab: Used for headings to give a strong, classic feel reminiscent of comic titles.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text to ensure readability and a clean, modern look.
