Superman on the Big Screen

Superman (1978)

Directed by Richard Donner, this film introduced Christopher Reeve as the definitive Superman. With its iconic score and heartfelt tone, it remains a classic.

Superman Returns (2006)

Brandon Routh stars in this spiritual sequel to the original films, exploring Superman’s return to Earth after a long absence.

Man of Steel (2013)

Directed by Zack Snyder, this reboot stars Henry Cavill and reimagines Superman’s origin with a modern, action-packed lens.

Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016)

Superman faces off against Batman in a dark, philosophical clash of ideals. The film explores sacrifice, fear, and redemption.
